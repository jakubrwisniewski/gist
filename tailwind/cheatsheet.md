# Tailwind Cheat Sheet

---

## 🎨 Colors
* **Text:** `text-red-500`, `text-gray-800`
* **Background:** `bg-blue-600`, `bg-indigo-700`
* **Border:** `border-green-400`
* **States:** `hover:bg-yellow-500`

---

## 💡 Flexbox
* **Display:** `flex`
* **Direction:** `flex-row`, `flex-col`
* **Justify Content:** `justify-start`, `justify-center`, `justify-end`
* **Align Items:** `items-start`, `items-center`, `items-end`
* **Behavior:** `flex-wrap`, `flex-nowrap`, `gap-4`

---

## 🔡 Typography
* **Font Size:** `text-xs`, `text-sm`, `text-base`, `text-lg`, `text-xl`, `text-2xl`, `text-4xl`
* **Font Weight:** `font-thin`, `font-light`, `font-normal`, `font-medium`, `font-bold`, `font-black`
* **Style:** `italic`, `not-italic`, `underline`, `line-through`
* **Transform:** `uppercase`, `lowercase`, `capitalize`

---

## 📐 Layout
* **Display:** `container`, `flex`, `grid`, `inline-block`, `hidden`, `block`, `inline-flex`
* **Alignment:** `mx-auto`, `items-center`, `justify-between`
* **Sizing:** `w-full`, `h-screen`, `max-w-md`, `min-h-[300px]`

---

## 📏 Spacing (Margin & Padding)

* **General:** `m-0`, `m-2`, `m-4`, `m-8`, `p-0`, `p-2`, `p-4`, `p-8`
* **Axis:** `mx-4` (left & right), `my-2` (top & bottom)
* **Directional:** `mt-4` (top), `mb-4` (bottom), `ml-4` (left), `mr-4` (right)

---

## 📍 Position
* **Type:** `relative`, `absolute`, `fixed`, `sticky`
* **Placement:** `top-0`, `bottom-0`, `left-0`, `right-0`
* **Z-Index:** `z-0`, `z-10`, `z-50`

---

## 🔳 Grid
* **Structure:** `grid`, `grid-cols-2`, `grid-cols-3`, `grid-cols-4`
* **Sizing:** `col-span-2`, `col-span-3`
* **Spacing:** `gap-2`, `gap-4`, `gap-8`

---

## 🌈 Backgrounds
* **Sizing/Repeat:** `bg-cover`, `bg-contain`, `bg-center`, `bg-no-repeat`
* **Gradients:** `bg-gradient-to-r`, `from-blue-500`, `to-purple-600`

---

## 🌫️ Shadows & Effects
* **Shadows:** `shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`, `shadow-lg`, `shadow-xl`, `shadow-2xl`, `shadow-inner`, `shadow-none`
* **Opacity:** `opacity-0`, `opacity-50`
